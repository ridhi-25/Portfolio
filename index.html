ABOUT ME


Hello! I'm Ridhi Sai Pasupuleti, currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at SRM University - AP. I am passionate about technology and innovation, with a keen interest in software development, artificial intelligence, and problem-solving.

In my free time, I enjoy expressing my creativity through drawing, which allows me to unwind and explore my artistic side. I believe in balancing technical skills with creative pursuits, as it helps me approach challenges with a unique perspective.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have a deep interest in exploring the intersection of art and technology. Drawing has been a lifelong passion of mine, allowing me to express my thoughts and emotions visually. I enjoy sketching, painting, and creating illustrations that reflect my imagination and creativity.

This hobby not only helps me relax but also enhances my attention to detail and patience, skills that are equally valuable in my technical endeavors. I am also fascinated by how technology can be used to create art, such as through generative design. I am always eager to learn, grow, and contribute to meaningful projects in the tech world.
